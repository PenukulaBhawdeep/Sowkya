About

AgriSmart is a web-based platform that delivers personalized fertilizer recommendations by assessing soil health and weather conditions, providing insights on optimal amounts and costs. It features disease and pest detection through image processing, enabling early diagnosis and management suggestions for farmers. The application also offers tailored crop recommendations based on environmental data and includes a chatbot for real-time support and AI-driven decision-making assistance.
